About My’Chal

I’m Mychal! Born and raised in Houston, Texas. Currently residing in Humble, Texas! I have always had a passion for capturing people in their happiest times or those awkward moments.

I have a love for forms of art and began shooting about two years ago. My specialty is outdoor photography, nature, portraits, and weddings. I’m a natural light photographer and believe that nothing is more precious than catching moments at their natural form. My goal is to capture those moments and let you visit them forever more!
