The Problem

Right now there is a community of high quality photographers in almost every city that is telling stunning visual stories. We're bridging the gap between people and their local creatives through creative design and fostering trust between people

Our Mission

It began with a photoshoot in the streets of Downtown Houston where some of the best photographers in the the city were creating amazing visual stories with their camera and their creativity.

We decided to build Shoot so that everyone in the world can have access to the best photographers in their own cities or wherever they choose to travel.
