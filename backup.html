About Lisa

Hey there! My name is Lisa Martinez and I am a beauty photographer based in Houston, Texas.

I have 8 years of experience with photography and just recently fell in love with beauty photography in particular.
I am completely self taught, learning from online tutorials and just plain 'ol practice. Since my images contain a high level of detail I have also had to learn how to retouch. Each and every one of my images are retouched by me.

I am available for commercial and personal assignments.
