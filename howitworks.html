Welcome to SHOOT!
Here’s how it works!

1. Find a photographer

Scroll through the website, and check out our selection of skilled photographers. Click on "View Profile" so you can look at their portfolio and package offerings.

2. Send a Booking Request

Once you have checked the photographer's portfolio and packages out, click on the "Book" button on the photogrphers page, which will take you to the booking section.

Choose a package from the options, and enter your contact information, so that your photographer and the SHOOT team can get a hold of you. We will reach out to you in less than 12 hours to confirm all the details and help you prepare everything that you will need to have for a perfect photoshoot. Then, your photographer will contact you to make the SHOOT happen!

3. Pay online!

Pay for your photoshoot online! No need to worry about deposits and proofs because SHOOT holds the payment until the photographer delivers all of your finished photos and the photos meet our standards.

4. Enjoy your photoshoot!

Meet up with your photographer. Relax, dress your best, and don’t forget to pack a smile.

You will receive all your photos from SHOOT via Google Drive.

Be sure to tag SHOOT in your Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, and LinkedIn posts for the chance to win a free photoshoot!
