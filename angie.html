About Angie

ANGELICA DUONG IS A WILD TRAVELER WITH A BIG APPETITE. SHE IS 20 YEARS OLD AND WAS BORN/RAISED IN HOUSTON, TX. ANGELICA IS A FULL TIME PHOTOGRAPHER/VIDEOGRAPHER.

SHE STARTED ON JANUARY 25TH OF 2017. EVER SINCE ANGELICA BOUGHT HER FIRST CAMERA IN AUGUST, IT WAS LIFE CHANGING. SHE STARTED TO EXPLORE MANY DIFFERENT STYLES; IT WAS AN ENDLESS ROUTE OF LEARNING TECHNIQUES. SHE ALWAYS STRIVES TO MAKE PEOPLE'S VISION COME TO LIFE.

THERE IS NOTHING THAT ANGELICA LOVES MORE THAN TALKING/MEETING NEW PEOPLE AND CAPTURING THE AUTHENTIC MOMENTS. IT IS AN HONOR WHEN A CLIENT CHOOSES HER TO TAKE THEIR PICTURES, BECAUSE SHE BELIEVES THAT SHE IS CREATING A WAY TO MAKE THAT HAPPY MEMORY BECOME PERMANENT.

PHOTOGRAPHY ALLOWS ANGELICA TO THINK OUTSIDE OF THE BOX AND MAKE SOMETHING OUT OF NOTHING - SO GET READY FOR A FUN TIME, RANDOM DATING, AND AN AWESOME ADVENTURE WITH ANGELICA DUONG.
