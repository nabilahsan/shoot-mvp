About Angie

Angelica Duong is a full time photographer and videographer, born and raised in Houston, TX.

She started on January of 2017. Ever since Angelica bought her first camera in August, it was life changing.
She started to explore many different styles; it was an endless route of learning techniques.

There is nothing that Angelica loves more than talking/meeting new people and capturing the authentic moments
It is an honor when a client chooses her to take their pictures, because she believes that she is
creating a way to make that happy memory permanent.

Photography allows Angelica to think outside of the box and make something out of nothing
- so get ready for a fun time, random dating, and an awesome adventure with Angelica Duong.
