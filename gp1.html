About Andre

I'm Andre, a Houston native who really enjoys capturing moments in time.

I first began doing photography about 5 years ago while I was a student at Prairie View A&M University, a member of the HBCU family. I really enjoy shooting photos for graduates and newly weds because these are special moments.

I'm also a natural-light photographer that believes there is beauty in imperfection. That wrinkle or that dimple that you don't like, is beautiful. Those freckles or that mole, is beautiful. So, I don't touch it. It's a part of who you are and I like to present YOU in the best light.
